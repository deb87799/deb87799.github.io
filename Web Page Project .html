My Favorite Things About UGA
Of course I enjoy the football at UGA and the other sports the school has to offer. However, I also enjoy the community of the school itself. Everyone is friendly to everyone and it creates a atmosphere that helps you feel at home. The campus was fairly easy to learn and it provides me with everything I would ever need to me succesful. Athens itself is a great college town and is big enough to satisfy all of my needs. Downtown has a wide variety of shops and restuarants that keep things from getting stale. I would not have wanted to go anywhere else, and I am very happy with my decision to come to God's favorite university.
